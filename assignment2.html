Forgotten Media

The internet is currently saturated with an immense variety of media catering to a vast array of interests and forms of entertainment. There is a many-to-many relationship between a creator and their media, and a many-to-many relationship between consumers and the media they consume. Are creators producing more than consumers could ever see in their lifetime? How much content never reaches any meaningfully large audience before it is forgotten? Yes, some content goes viral, seen by millions, but what else is lost in the noise?
